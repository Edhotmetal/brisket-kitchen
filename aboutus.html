    2. The Brisket Kitchen has been family owned since 1979.

  2. The Owners

    Owners Leon and Shelby have been passionate about smoked and grilled delicacies ever since they first saw their father build a smokehouse on the family farm. Since then, the Hornady family has been enjoying Thanksgiving Brisket in lieu of Turkey and has never looked back! In 1978, Leon and Shelby decided to bring the joy of Thanksgiving brisket to their local community. It was such a hit that they opened their own restaurant the very next year.
